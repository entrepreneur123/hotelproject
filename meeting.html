The Art Of Seastreak Meetings and Events

Meetings and events at Seastreak are an artful experience. From executive strategy sessions and private dinners to creative team-building events and international conferences, our expert staff will ensure a highly memorable business or social gathering.

Idyllic Surroundings

Seastreak offers spaces in superb locations, whether you seek an urban Spanish Colonial Revival oasis in Beverly Hills, an oceanfront Craftsman-style retreat in Laguna Beach or an on-mountain getaway in Park City, Utah. We offer indoor and outdoor venues from intimate to grand, all with a unique sense of place in some of the world’s most preferred locations.

Culinary Artistry

We have a deep appreciation for fine food and wine, and our talented chefs and sommelier teams are true culinary artists. Let them design inventive menus for your gatherings that showcase the freshest regional seasonal ingredients paired with selections from our extensive wine cellars or your choice of signature cocktails or fresh juices and smoothies. Private dining venues include signature Chef’s Tables, private dining rooms, estate-quality ballrooms, manicured event lawns and more.
